Key

This is the key for the Marvel Chronology Project (MCP). The MCP is an attempt to not only catalog every canonical appearance by every significant Marvel character, but to place those appearances in proper chronological order.

I owe a debt of gratitude to George Olshevsky (my personal hero), Murray Ward, and Peter Sanderson for laying the framework of this chronology in the various incarnations of the Marvel Index; however, none of them was personally involved in this, and any mistakes contained herein are entirely my own.

We have tried to maintain the chronology set out in the Marvel Indexes, but as you can tell by reading their errata sections, they are not mistake free. At times, we had no choice but to disagree with the Indexes. Wherever possible, we've tried to keep that to a minimum.

If your favorite character isn't listed--although, trust us, your favorite character would have to be pretty minor not to be listed--send us their appearances and we'll add them, but we reserve the right to readjust the chrono order.

Note that this is not the Marvel Appearances Project, and we are not attempting to list every appearance--just every quantifiable appearance (one that can be placed in a character's chronology) in every canonical story.

If you believe we've made a mistake in a character's chronology (i.e., appearances out of order), we look forward to reading your corrections, but please be prepared to back up your theory with relevant facts. To propose a change in a character's chronology, visit the Posting Board.

Russ Chappell
